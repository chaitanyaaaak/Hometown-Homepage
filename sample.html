Discover the Heart of Malwa

Indore, located in the heart of Madhya Pradesh, is a vibrant city that beautifully blends its rich history with a bustling, modern culture. It has been consistently ranked as India's cleanest city, a testament to its civic pride.

Famous for its culinary scene, historical monuments, and welcoming people, Indore offers a unique experience for every visitor. From the majestic Rajwada Palace to the sizzling street food of Sarafa Bazaar, the city is a treasure trove of sights, sounds, and flavors.

Must-Visit Places

Rajwada Palace

A magnificent historical palace that showcases a blend of Maratha, Mughal, and French architectural styles. It stands as a symbol of the Holkar dynasty's grandeur.

Sarafa Bazaar

A jeweler's market by day that transforms into a bustling street food paradise at night. A must-visit for every food lover to taste iconic Indori dishes.

Chappan Dukan (56 Shops)

Another iconic food street offering 56 different shops with a wide array of snacks, sweets, and beverages. It's a hub of culinary activity from morning till late night.

Things To Do

🍽️

Indulge in Street Food

From Poha Jalebi for breakfast to Garadu and Bhutte ka Kees in the evening, explore the unique flavors of Indore.

🏛️

Explore History

Visit historical marvels like Lal Bagh Palace and Kanch Mandir to witness the city's rich heritage and art.

🛍️

Shop at Local Markets

Wander through the bustling lanes of markets like MT Cloth Market and Sitlamata Bazaar for traditional textiles and crafts.
